Your Rights

When voting on our blockchain platform, you have several rights designed to ensure a fair and accessible electoral process. Firstly, you have the right to a secure and transparent voting experience, with each ballot recorded immutably on the blockchain. Secondly, your privacy is protected, and your personal information remains confidential throughout the voting process. Additionally, you have the right to verify that your vote has been accurately recorded and counted, thanks to the transparency of blockchain technology. Finally, you have the right to equal participation, as our platform is designed to be accessible to all eligible voters, regardless of background or circumstance. We are committed to upholding these rights and empowering you to exercise your democratic voice securely and confidently.
Blockchain Disclosure

Our voting platform utilizes a blockchain framework, specifically Ethereum, renowned for its security and transparency features. With Ethereum, each vote is cryptographically secured and stored across a distributed network of computers, ensuring tamper-proof records that are immutable and transparent. Importantly, your personal information remains private throughout the voting process. We employ advanced encryption techniques and pseudonymous identifiers, safeguarding your identity while still allowing you to participate in the democratic process securely. Rest assured that our commitment to privacy and security is paramount, guaranteeing a trustworthy and inclusive voting experience for all.
